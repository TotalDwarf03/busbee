Data Constraints, Limitations and Inconsistencies

Unfortunately, this visualisation highlights the inconsistencies in the datasets. On the map, you will notice that the bus route does not follow the actual path of the bus as it travels along roads.

This is because the route dataset (which does follow the roads) is not ordered correctly in line with the timetables and stop sequences. As a result, the visualisation connects the bus stops in a straight line, rather than following the actual roads.

To compensate for this, I have plotted the real route on the map in green, while the bus's movement is shown in blue. This way, you can see the intended path of the bus, even though the visualisation does not accurately reflect the bus's journey along the roads.

There is information within the dataset documentation that suggests a way of fixing this inconsistency, but I have not yet implemented this due to coursework time constraints.
